Volkov Manor

Where we put the "super" in "supernatural".

Welcome to Our Domain

This is the digital home of the Hybrid Family, an organization which, until recently, was hidden from the minds of ordinary humans. It is a safe haven for all manner of creatures, but was founded for the protection of hybrids, those that come from many but belong to none.

Origin and Purpose

  1. Volkov Manor, once home to a renowned scholar, is now the residence of the elusive Hybrid Family.
  2. Within its walls, creatures from all corners of the world (and some from beyond its borders) live in peace and serenity.
  3. This harmony is the result of mutual trust between cryptids, mythics, hybrids, and enlightened humans.

Safety and Conduct

Font of Knowledge

As a gesture of respect for the academic triumphs of the Manor's previous owner, the Hybrid Family offers an array of educational programs for both young and Awakened residents.
In the spirit of Master Volkov, these scholarly experiences have been advanced to the rank of Ivy League.
